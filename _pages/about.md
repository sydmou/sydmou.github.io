<p>
    <font size="3">
        I am a Ph.D student at the University of Sains Malaysia, where I was supervised by Dr. Cheah Yu-N. My research centers around Sentiment Analysis (ABSA), LLMs (Security, Alignment), Agent, RAG. I have been developing various sentiment analysis pre-trained language models and building an ACOSQE Sentiment Analysis model for the sentiment analysis field. In the past, I have gained industry experience at companies such as iHealth Labs, Lennove, and Vivaneo. I am currently a lecturer in Nomarl Education Center, Cangzhou Normal University. At the same time, I was a Ph.D student in <a href="">NLP Lab</a>, School of Computer Science, University of Sains Malaysia, working with <a href="https://www.chuatatseng.com/">Associte Prof. Cheah Yu-N</a>.
